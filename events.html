Thompson Falls

October 27th, 2023

A night of potent potions and enchanting elixirs inspired by the wizarding world! Guests learned to craft classic cocktails with a magical twist, enjoyed themed decorations, and competed in trivia for fantastic prizes. Butterbeer martinis were a huge hit!

Fort Jesus

August 12th, 2023

Gears whirred and spirits flowed at our Steampunk Soiree! We explored Victorian-era cocktail techniques using modern ingredients. Guests donned their finest goggles and top hats while enjoying inventive cocktails like the "Clockwork Orange" and the "Aeroplier Aperitif".

Mystical Mixology at Mt Kenya

June 3rd, 2023

A sensory journey into the art of cocktail creation. We explored the power of aroma, texture, and visual presentation to elevate the drinking experience. Highlights included molecular gastronomy techniques and the creation of personalized cocktail profiles.

Coastal Tiki Cocktail Paradise

May 15th, 2023

Escape to a tropical paradise with our Tiki cocktail night! We delved into the history and techniques of classic Tiki cocktails, from Mai Tais to Zombies. Guests enjoyed fruity concoctions, island-inspired decorations, and a taste of summer bliss. Ukulele music added to the ambiance!
